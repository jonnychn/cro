What the numbers mean

Visitors: number of people coming to your page.

Paying Customers: expected subscribers based on your traffic and conversion rate.

Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR): predictable monthly revenue from those customers.

Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR): yearly subscription revenue calculated from MRR.

Advertising Spend (Monthly): what you budget for ads each month.

Advertising Spend (Annual): yearly ad spend assuming the monthly budget stays the same.

Cost Per Click: advertising spend divided by visitors.

Payback Period: months needed to recover your advertising spend.

Profit Margin: percent of revenue kept after non-advertising costs.

Profit (Monthly): revenue remaining each month after ad spend and your chosen margin.

Profit (Annual): yearly profit if monthly results continue.
